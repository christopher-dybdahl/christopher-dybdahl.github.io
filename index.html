Analyzing integrated vs. mixed factor allocation strategies based on Leippold & Rueegg (2017). Five factors and four portfolio construction techniques on US equity data (1962-2024), with hypothesis testing on Sharpe ratios.

NTNU Master’s thesis on stochastic control for optimal execution under renewable-driven uncertainty, developing FBSDE-based numerical methods for finite-horizon diffusion control with market frictions, including neural approximations of non-linear HJB solutions.
